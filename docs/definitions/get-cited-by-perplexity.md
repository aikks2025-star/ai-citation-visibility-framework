# How Businesses Get Cited by Perplexity

**Definition:** Businesses get cited by Perplexity by leading with concise answers, citing sources visibly, keeping pages fresh, and making claims easy for Perplexity to retrieve, verify, synthesize, and link.

Perplexity citations are not controlled by the publisher. The practical goal is to become the clearest, freshest, best-sourced page for the prompt and then measure whether Perplexity actually cites it.

## Why it matters

Perplexity is a priority citation platform because citations are visible and measurable. That makes it a strong proving ground for whether answer blocks, provenance, and methodology links reduce citation friction.

## Measurement method

Rerun fixed prompts and record cited URLs, citation domains, response text, brand mentions, competitor mentions, timestamps, and response hashes.

# How Businesses Get Cited by ChatGPT

**Definition:** Businesses get cited by ChatGPT by publishing clear, self-contained answer blocks backed by entity clarity, original evidence, current sourcing, and corroborating third-party proof.

ChatGPT citations cannot be bought or guaranteed. The framework treats ChatGPT citation work as a repeatable evidence and measurement system, not a one-time content trick.

## Why it matters

Many ChatGPT optimization lists stop at tactics. The missing layer is measurement: whether a fixed prompt panel actually produces citations, mentions, omissions, or competitor displacement over time.

## Measurement method

Run a fixed prompt panel and record prompt text, model settings, response text, cited URLs, brand mentions, ghost mentions, competitor mentions, timestamps, and response hashes.

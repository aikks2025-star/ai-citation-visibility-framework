# Shortlist Economy

**Definition:** The shortlist economy is the market condition created when AI assistants answer with a short, curated set of recommended options instead of a long list of links.

The term explains the practical risk of AI-mediated discovery: if a brand is not in the AI-generated shortlist, it may be invisible even when it still has a website, ads, and organic rankings.

## Why it matters

AI systems compress discovery. Instead of showing ten links or dozens of providers, an answer may name only a small set of options. Brands outside that shortlist lose consideration before the buyer reaches a website.

## Measurement method

Track whether a brand is absent, mentioned, cited, recommended, or compared across recurring category prompts. Measure Share of Model, citation URL presence, competitor mentions, and prompt-level shortlist inclusion.

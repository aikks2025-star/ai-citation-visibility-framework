# Ads in AI Search

**Definition:** Ads in AI search can appear near or inside AI-era search experiences, but paid placement should not be treated as the same thing as organic AI recommendation or citation selection.

For ChatGPT specifically, OpenAI states that ads run on separate systems and advertisers cannot shape, rank, or alter organic responses. Other platforms may use different ad formats and should be evaluated separately.

## Why it matters

AI search changes the budget question. If the answer itself is earned through citable evidence and entity clarity, media spend cannot replace the work of becoming a trusted source.

## Measurement method

Track whether ads are present separately from whether the brand is organically cited, mentioned, recommended, or absent in the response.

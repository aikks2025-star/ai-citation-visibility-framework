# Agentic Commerce for Marketing Visibility

**Definition:** Agentic commerce is a model of buying and selling where AI agents help users research, compare, choose, and sometimes purchase products or services with minimal human steps.

For marketing visibility, the important question is not only what agentic commerce is. The important question is whether an AI agent can identify, verify, compare, and recommend a brand before the human buyer sees a shortlist.

## Why it matters

Agentic commerce shifts brand discovery from page-one ranking toward machine-readable selection. Products, services, and local businesses need structured facts, citable claims, current source proof, and entity clarity.

## Measurement method

Test whether AI systems can identify, compare, cite, and recommend a brand across fixed commercial prompts. Record citations, mentions, omissions, competitor recommendations, and source URLs.

# AI Citation Decay

**Definition:** AI citation decay is the loss or rotation of a brand's citations inside AI answers over time as systems continuously re-retrieve, re-rank, and replace sources.

AI citation decay is not a fixed platform rule or guaranteed timeline. The AI Citation Visibility Framework treats it as a measurable risk pattern driven by index churn, model churn, competition, and canonical drift.

## Why it matters

AI answers can become the first buyer touchpoint. If a business stops being selected as a source, it can lose visibility before analytics show a normal search decline.

## Measurement method

Measure citation decay by rerunning a fixed prompt panel and recording cited URLs, brand mentions, omitted brands, competitor mentions, timestamps, model settings, and response hashes.

MAHI-100 provides the reproducible capture structure. MAD-M provides the governance lens for review, provenance, and refresh cycles.
